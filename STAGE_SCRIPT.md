## Judge Q&A — answers ready

**Q: "Why won't Motion build this themselves?"**
> "Motion is a workflow orchestrator for sub-100 person teams that already have Slack and Salesforce. To serve Sofía they'd have to throw away their architecture — they're built on top of customer SaaS, not under it. We're a phone number and a DM scraper. Different shape, different customer."

**Q: "Isn't this too broad?"**
> "We start with two verticals — hair salons and florists in San Francisco. Both have high-relationship customers, heavy DM inbound, no existing tech stack, and zero patience for onboarding. Three pilots verbal. Use them to prove unit economics, then lateral."

**Q: "Bond already calls themselves an AI Chief of Staff."**
> "Bond serves CEOs of funded companies — people who already *have* a Chief of Staff or three. We serve the owner who *is* the Chief of Staff, the receptionist, the bookkeeper, and the stylist. We renamed it 'co-founder' because that's what these owners need: a peer, not staff."

**Q: "Where does GBrain specifically help vs. context-stuffing a prompt?"**
> "GBrain holds the operating memory across years. Sofía asks 'when did Adriana last cancel?' — that's a four-month-back lookup. Prompt context can't do that. And critically — Sofía owns the GBrain. If we shut down tomorrow she keeps every DM, every booking, every transformation photo. That's the moat."

**Q: "What if the AI hallucinates and books the wrong wedding?"**
> "Approval gates. Pop drafts everything. Owner taps to send. We earn autonomy over time — same way you'd trust a new front desk hire. Daily false-positive rate is tracked in the dashboard."

**Q: "$99/mo — is that high or low?"**
> "Salon owners pay $400/mo for a part-time receptionist who answers a fraction of the inbound. Pop replies to 100% of DMs, books appointments, and sends invoice reminders. We need to save them 25% of one receptionist hour to be free. We save them ten."

**Q: "Three pilots — are they signed?"**
> "Verbal LOIs. Sol & Trenza on Mission, Mariposa Florist on Valencia, and Coco Studio on 24th. We onboarded by walking in with a laptop. Setup time: 12 minutes — we forward their Instagram, read their inbox, done."

## Backup plans

- **TTS fails:** Press SPACE through the voice phases anyway — captions still display. Just narrate Pop's answer reading off the screen.
- **WiFi fails:** All cached responses ship in the static bundle. Demo loads even offline.
- **3D flip breaks (Safari):** Hit `/full` and walk through the long version manually.
- **Judge asks for a live demo:** Pull up `/full#clone`, type their business in, Pop builds the AI-native version of it.
